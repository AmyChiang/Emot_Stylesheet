Overview

“Wee Express is an app for kids to learn their emotions and facial expressions with lots of fun.” Emotion, or feeling is always an important topic, but people can only learn by themselves. From the exposed research, it shows that the emotion development in the childhood would cause a serious affect on emotion management after they grew up.

The prime time for kids to learn emotions in from 3 to 6 because of the improvement and social skill requirement after this age. Kids in this age start to experience more sophisticated emotions, but still using relatively limited vocabulary to express themselves

Considering the emotions related apps for young kids in the market still has a lot of room for improvement, so the goal of Emot app is helping kids to explore their feelings, how to deal with it and learn some emotion related vocabulary through games and animations. All the illustrations, animations, interactions and games would catch kids' eyes and encourage them to explore their expressions.
